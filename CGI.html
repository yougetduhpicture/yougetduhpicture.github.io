Internshop at Finanssi-Kontio(CGI)

  • Time: Spring 2023
  • Team: 10 people + Offshore 6 people
  • My role: Java-Trainee

What did I do?

My mission was to get to know the work culture of Finanssi-Kontio and the meetings and such. I did some tracing in Git and solve production log errors and was able to perform these tasks with the help of my workmates. Also, I went through various company working etiquette training sessions to learn about cyber security, how to be prepared for information leaks and how to handle confidential information. My confidence in spoken English garnered from working in the UK music business proved to be very useful when communicating with offshore workers from India.

Tools

Command prompt, Docker, Eclipse, Teams, Virtual desktop, Jira, Confluence, Outlook, Teragrep, Splunk, Excel

Getting to Know the Work Culture

I was very warmly welcomed from the very beginning. Everybody was very friendly and I got to know the people of my team, few of which I already knew before from school. A daily meeting and a technical meeting almost everyday. We also had demo sessions on Friday. Those sessions displayed what each team had been doing, so that everybody could keep track of what's happening with the service we were working on. A fundamental part of the culture was that everybody should help everybody, if they ask for help. This made it possible to learn the unwritten knowledge and the in's and out's of different ways to solve the problems.

Feedback

The people at CGI were pleased with my work, but unfortunately we came to a conclusion that the work as an IT-consultant wasn't too suitable for my life situation at that moment, since I needed to be away quite a lot to tour with my band and the remote work from abroad countries wasn't possible in this company.
